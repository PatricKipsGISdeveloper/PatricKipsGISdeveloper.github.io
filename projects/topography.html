Project Description:

The Project involves utilization of characteristics that can be derived directly from digital elevation Model(DEM) to analyse critical areas within the catchment through prioritization

Project Objectives:

  • Utilize Morphometric analysis in prioritization of Subwatersheds
  • Compare the Results of Subwatershed Prioritization Using SWAT model and Morphometric Analysis using SWPT(validate)

Study Area:

The Study area:The Yala river basin is located in western parts of Kenya and drain into Lake Victoria in Siaya County ,River Yala originates in Nandi Escarpment in Rift Valley and flows west for 219 kilometers to its Mouth in Lake Victoria .River Yala has an average discharge of 27.4 cubic meters per second..
